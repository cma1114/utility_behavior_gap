# Feature Directionality by Contrast

A feature-task row is directionally positive when the arm gap points in the direction associated with better panel scores: `gap_effect * panel_coef_per_sd > 0`. For moral-low vs framed-empty, positive means positive for the harmful side; negative means positive for framed-empty. CIs are Wilson/binomial intervals over feature-task rows and should be read as descriptive uncertainty over the feature set, not trial-level inference.
